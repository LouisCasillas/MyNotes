Basic website must haves

    • Add at least one embedded video per page.
        ◦ Show users using the product.
    • Show related content like similar posts
        ◦ If the content is very relevant you can put it higher up
            ▪ “Recommended for You”
    • Have a nice navigation menu
    • Have an index page
    • Have an About page
        ◦ List all software/services used and give affiliate links to the software/services like SEMRush
    • Have a cookie warning.
        ◦ “No cookies!  We respect your privacy and will never track you!”
    • Make the website responsive to different screen sizes and devices
    • Let users engage through comments or ratings/likes/up-down-votes? Might not be worth the effort.

    • Have a way to gather user e-mail addresses or information

        ◦ Possibly use e-mail campaign services like MailChimp
        ◦ Use form services
            ▪ https://blog.webjeda.com/jekyll-contact-form/
        ◦

    • Make sure the page is authoritative (EAT)
